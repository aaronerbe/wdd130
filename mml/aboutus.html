ABOUT US

Gateway To The Backcountry

We love Idaho living where we're able to enjoy all the outdoors offers. We are blessed to live on a meadow in the mountains outside of the Boise area. Our community borders up to a nearly endless trailsystem that winds towards the Sawtooth Mountain Ranges. We love to explore the Idaho backcountry rich in history of abandoned and active mines and are always searching for the hidden vista views it has to offer.

Beyond The Backyard

Living in the mountains at the edge of the grid has it's challenges and requires the whole family coming together to work hard. It provides opportunities for our children to learn the craft and skills that will benefit them through their lives. Just as important to us is to get away and experience adventures to create positive memories as a family to create bonds that will last.
